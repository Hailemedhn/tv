How to install Kora

Kora works on Android mobile devices and compatible Android TV devices, including Android TV sticks and Android TV boxes.

Android Mobile Phone

  1. Tap the Download App button above.
  2. Open the downloaded APK file.
  3. If asked, allow installation from your browser or file manager.
  4. Tap Install and then open Kora.

Android TV Stick / Android TV Box

  1. Open a browser or downloader app on your Android TV device.
  2. Go to this webpage.
  3. Select Download App using your remote control.
  4. Open the downloaded APK file.
  5. If prompted, enable installation from unknown sources for your browser or downloader app.
  6. Select Install, then open Kora from your apps list.
For Android TV devices, you may need to enable APK installation in: Settings → Security & Restrictions → Unknown Sources. The exact wording may be different depending on your TV stick or TV box.

Before installing

  • Make sure your device is connected to the internet.
  • Use an Android TV stick or Android TV box with enough free storage.
  • If the app does not appear on the TV home screen, open it from the full apps list or app manager.
